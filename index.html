    Rowdy Rebel, hailing from the vibrant streets of New York, is a dynamic force in the hip-hop scene.

    Known for his raw authenticity and gritty lyricism, he brings an unparalleled energy that captivates audiences worldwide.

    Rowdy Rebel gained widespread recognition as a key member of the GS9 collective, alongside Bobby Shmurda. His breakout moment came with the viral success of "Hot N*gga, " a track that became an anthem and showcased his distinctive style

    Despite facing legal challenges, Rowdy Rebel's influence continued to grow, solidifying his status as an authentic voice in hip-hop.

    Scrptiid

     | Scrptiid • jadeentmia@gmail.com

    Romario O’Shane Hanson (born September 23, 1994), also known as Scrptiid , is a singer, songwriter, poet and aspiring actor. He is a native of Spanish Town, St. Catherine, Jamaica. Scrptiid’s first love was poetry, which was inspired by his uncle who passed away when Scrptiid was just 14 years old. He, then, befriended his late uncle’s friend, Teflon. In the bedroom studio with Teflon and Chronixx birthed the idea of artist name “ Scrptiid ”.

    Other artists in which Scrptiid is relatedly & relatively influenced by are Vybz Kartel , Movado, Wizkid and Burna Boy. While Scrptiid doesn’t restrict himself to any one genre, he crafts his music by infusing the sounds of dancehall, reggae, afro - pop, rock, and more

     
     
     

    Don Q

       |  Don Q • chosenfewrecords@hotmail.de 

    Don Q, is an American rapper. He is best known for his collaborations with labelmate A Boogie wit da Hoodie.

    Don signed to Highbridge the Label, a label ran by friends Quincy "QP" Acheampong and Sambou "Bubba" Camara. He and A Boogie were the first artists signed to the label. He was heavily featured on the compilation, Highbridge the Label: The Takeover Vol. 1, which dropped on May 28, 2016. Don gained more notoriety after appearing on Funkmaster Flex's freestyle series on Hot 97, along with A Boogie wit da Hoodie who had become one of the most popular up and coming rappers in New York City.[

    Don dropped his first official mixtape in August 2016. The mixtape titled Don Season was hosted by DJ Don Cannon. The mixtape has features from A Boogie, PnB Rock and Keir. In September 2016, he was featured on the BET Hip Hop Awards cypher alongside A Boogie wit da Hoodie, Russ, Kent Jones and Nick Grant. During the year, Don collaborated with artists like Meek Mill, Dave East, Fabolous, Young Scooter and PnB Rock among others.
